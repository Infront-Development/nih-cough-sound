{% trans "PURPOSE:" %}
{% blocktrans %} In this study we will collect breath and cough sounds sample from healthy individuals, COVID-19 patients, and individuals with other infectious diseases. These samples will be then used to develop the Artificial Intelligence (AI) model to distinguish the difference of diseases through cough and breath sounds. Total number of participants is estimated to be 2400 for each type of disease. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "STUDY PROCEDURES:" %}
{% blocktrans %} Once you have agreed to participate in this study, you are required to answer few questions about yourself. After that, you are required to record and submit your cough and breath sounds using the recording function through the website. The following are the questions that are needed in this study: {% endblocktrans %}

  1. {% trans "Respondent Status" %}
  2. {% trans "Sex" %}
  3. {% trans "Age" %}
  4. {% trans "Medical conditions" %}
  5. {% trans "Smoking Status" %}
  6. {% trans "Symptoms" %}
  7. {% trans "Date of diagnosis or test" %}
{% blocktrans %} The total participation time should be around 10 minutes.

You will record your breath and cough sound using the recording capability in this website and the and the sound will be captured and sent to the study team and only be accessed by them for the purpose of research only.

At the end of the recording, we will ask for your approximate location as well. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "RISK, PRECAUTIONS & POTENTIAL DISCOMFORT:" %}
{% blocktrans %} Make sure no other persons are within 20 feet of you when recording your breath and cough. Make sure you are away from any source of noises to avoid unclear or contaminated recordings. You may experience minimal discomfort when producing cough. However, this will not cause any harm to you. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "POTENTIAL BENEFITS:" %}
{% blocktrans %} By participating in this research study, you will contribute to solve a very important world problem. Eventually, your participation will contribute to the development of an automated infectious diseases screening web tool. {% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "CONFIDENTIALITY & PRIVACY:" %}

{% blocktrans %} Information from you will be limited to breath and cough recordings and answers to the above questions. All data from the study will be stored in a secure local data server where they will be protected with encryption at all times, only accessible to participating researchers and organizations. We will keep a repository of the data after the study is completed so that we can use it for development of similar Artificial Intelligence, for example, if another pandemic appears in the future. {% endblocktrans %}
